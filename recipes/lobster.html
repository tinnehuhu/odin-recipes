Lobster Rolls

This lobster roll recipe is a wonderful summer (or winter) treat and makes a fancy lunch that will impress your friends.

Ingredients

  • Buttered rolls
  • Vegetables
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lime juice
  • Seasonings
  • Lobster

Directions

  1. Butter the roll and line with lettuce.
  2. Make the sauce and fold in the lobster
  3. Stuff the lobster mixture into buns and top with dried herbs
